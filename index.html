Meet the Punks

The CryptoPunks are 24x24 pixel art images, generated algorithmically. Most are punky-looking guys and girls, but there are a few rarer types mixed in: Apes, Zombies and even the odd Alien. Every punk has there own profile page that shows their attributes as well as their ownership/for-sale status.

Punks with a blue background are not for sale and have no current bids.

Punks with a red background are available for sale by their owner.

Punks with a purple background have an active bid on them
